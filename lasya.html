We invite you to be a part of Lasya, the dance extravaganza at Pravega-2017, the annual fest of the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. Here you get to compete with teams just as passionate and talented as yours, but with strikingly distinct dance forms. So, let no strict aesthetic contain you in your endeavour to leave the audience awestruck with your style. So, get ready to dream with your feet, recite with your soul, speak with your expressions and to dance your heart out!

There are cash prizes worth 50K. So, gear up and start practising!

Rules

  • Each team can have minimum of 4 and maximum of 20 members.
  • Any member of dance groups is permitted from an institution. Members of one team need not necessarily be from same institution.
  • Semi-pro dance groups are allowed to participate. The competition is not restricted to the students of various institutions.
  • No participant can participate as a member of more than one team.
  • Any number of teams are allowed from a region. Borders are absolutely disregarded in judgement criteria.
  • Prelims:
    • A team should upload it's video and audio files in an exclusive folder (named as that of your dance group) in Google Drive and share the folder to Lasya team using the folder link in the appropriate column in the registration form. The audio file for the video also has to be separately uploaded alongside the video, so that it can be layered with video file, if required. The duration of video and audio files should be same. The video should be a single frame video. The camera should be fixed in one place. No zoom in or zoom out is allowed.
    • Time length of video: 3 to 6 minutes
    • Resolution: 720px(minimum)
    • Movements and formations of all the participants should be visible distinctly.
    • Participants are advised not to use heavy props as those are not allowed in finals
    • Vulgarity in any form is strictly prohibited. Use of water, fire or any inflammable substance is prohibited
    • Videos of past performances are acceptable if they fulfill the other criteria.
    • Costumes are not necessary. They don't carry extra points. But it is preferable if everyone from a team perform in uniform clothing.
    • You will be required to mention the name of your team, name and photos (for identification) of all the members, contact details, etc, in the registration portal.
    • The judgement criterion will be based on
      1. Creativity, theme and innovation
      2. Choreography and coordination
      3. Song Selection
      4. Elegance and style
  • Finals:
    • Teams must submit their tracks at least one and half hours before the event in a pendrive and cd in MP3 format and are requested to keep backup. Live music is not allowed.
    • The following particulars are to be written on the CD:
      1. Name of the team leader.
      2. Contact number.
      3. Tag the same in the pendrive.
    • Time length of performance: 5 to 10 minutes.
    • The stage is 28ft by 36ft. So teams should prepare their final performance keeping this in mind
    • All participants should carry proper ID proof.
    • The order of on-stage appearances will be decided based on a lottery that will be conducted backstage before the event begins on the final day.
    • In addition to the maximum permissible number of dancers, each team can have maximum of three non-dancers for music, lights, narration, stage management, etc.
    • 5mins of additional time will be given for setting up the stage or props. Teams should themselves clear the stage for the next team. (Props include objects carried by participants, such as sticks, swords, pots, etc. Chairs, tables, screens and backdrops come under stage settings.)
    • Vulgarity in any form is strictly prohibited.
    • Use of water, fire or any other inflammable substances is prohibited. Violation of rules will lead to disqualification.
    • The decision of the judges are taken as final and no objections or questions will be entertained in this regard.
    • The judgement criterion will be based on
      1. Creativity, theme and innovation
      2. Choreography and coordination
      3. Costumes, Song Selection and Properties
      4. Stage presence and style
      5. Energy level and overall impact on crowd

About Lasya

Lasya is a national group dance competition, organised as part of Pravega,the annual fest of Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. It provides a stage with no boundaries and no categories. Here, all dance styles are welcome and are equal. Teams will get to expand their horizons as there are no strict aesthetics to contain them. So , come and embrace the oppurtunity to leave the audience awestruck with your powerful choreographies, elegance and style.
