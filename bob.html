One of the leading fests in the country, Pravega brings to you a nationwide band competition for semi-pro bands with Prelims in six cities - Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Hyderabad and Bangalore.
In this mega event, a lot is up for grabs - Huge Cash Prizes, direct entry to MTV Xtreme, recording deals, scholarship opportunities and exclusive features in music journals for the winner and the runners-up. Selected bands get to perform in our first pro-nite in front of an 8000+ music-crazy crowd of Bangalore, and open for a leading national pro band.

×

General Rules & Regulations

  1. All sorts of bands are allowed to participate, whether it consists of college students or not.
  2. There will be no language restrictions on the entries.
  3. The band will only be considered if they send us a link to one of their tracks on either soundcloud.com or reverbnation.com.
  4. This is a 3 Stage contest, where screening round will be conducted online, based on the link of the track(s) the band sends us, followed by a set of live events which will be held in the various venues we partner with, and finally the finale which will be held the course of the festival at IISc Bangalore, India
  5. Pravega will have exclusive rights to use any of the music tracks for future promotional purposes, although due credit to the band will always be given.
  6. Any dispute arising due to any confusion regarding the contest shall be resolved only by IISc Bangalore and the Battle of the Bands team and the decision or resolution provided will be considered final.
×

Rules for Online Screening

  1. Bands across the country submit a link to one or two of their tracks on youtube.com, soundcloud.com or reverbnation.com . Depending on the number of registrations, any number of bands from 5 to 8 shall be selected from each city.
  2. The link to the song should preferably be that of an original composition, although covers are also acceptable. Please note that the selections will be based on the quality of your music and not on whether the track is original. However, an original track always reflects the composition skills of the band in question.
×

Rules for Regional Prelims

  1. We shall select anything between 5 to 8 bands from each city for the city prelims at the specified venue depending on the number of registrations. The bands will be judged live by our team members and we shall also record the performance somewhere for later listening by our judges in Bangalore.
  2. Each band will get 20+5 minutes for their performance in the prelims, which includes sound check. However, bands will be given a total of half an hour before the prelims begin to have a preliminary master sound balancing. The bands can perform as many tracks as they want to in that time limit.
×

Rules for the Finale

  1. Top 5 Bands from all across the country shall battle it out at the final competitive night in IISc, Bangalore in front of the first pro-nite crowd on 20th January.
  2. Bands shall be judged by eminent musicians of the country. This is the last stage which shall involve any type of judgement. Results will be announced at the end of the headlining act.
  3. Bands will have to bring their own musical instruments and effects. However programmed music is not allowed. Standard drum set will be provided, along with standard live performance setup with amps, monitor, mixer, DI boxes, line array speakers, etc.
  4. Each band will be given 30(25 + 5) minutes for their performance in which also includes the sound check.
  5. Each band will be allowed to perform as many tracks as they want within the stipulated time limit.
  6. Pravega, IISc Bangalore reserves the right to make any changes in the rules, without any prior notice and disqualify any participating band on grounds of misconduct or obscene behavior.
  7. A top national band will headline the Battle of the Bands event in Pravega on 20th. The details of the prizes will be given in our page.
