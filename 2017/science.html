Rules

  • Each team will consist of 2 persons.
  • Prelims: There will be general questions based on strategies. 9 teams will be selected based on Preliminary round.
    It will take about 2 hours
  • Finals:
    • In the beginning each team will be given a fixed amount of money and will not be supplied with extra money during the course of the game.
    • The game will be conducted in rounds. In each round, a particular number of goods will be auctioned.
    • The goods are auctioned as follows:
      1. Each team will write down their bid for the good which will not be disclosed to the rest of the teams.
      2. The highest bidder will be buying the good but at the price of the second highest bidder.
      3. In case of tie of the highest bidders, the tie will be resolved by re-bidding among the multiple winners. In this scenario, the winners will have to bid more than the highest common bid.
      4. If a team if forced to pay more than the money it has, the team will be declared disqualified.
    • At the end of each round, the value of money one possesses will decrease.
    • When a person buys a good, it has a fixed value. This prices changes from round to round ,i.e, the price of the goods will change with successive rounds.
    • The value a team will have is the sum of the value of the money he has and the value of the goods he possesses.
    • The value of the goods with a team will increase in each round.
    • The objective of the game is to maximize the total value of the team. At the end of the five rounds, the team with the highest value wins.
    • It will last about three hours.
